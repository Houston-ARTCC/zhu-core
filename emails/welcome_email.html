Discord

We use Discord as our primary text communications platform. All ARTCC announcements are made on our Discord server and this is also where you can chat with other controllers. You can download Discord at https://discord.com.

Please join our Discord server by clicking this link or using the invite code Ag2cdZz. If you would like to have your controller rating automatically synced, you must configure VATSIM Sync at https://vatsimsync.com.

TeamSpeak

TeamSpeak is our primary voice communications platform. While on the network, all controllers must be in a TeamSpeak channel for coordination. TeamSpeak 3 can be download at https://www.teamspeak.com/en.

You can connect to our server by using the IP ts.zhuartcc.org. Use your full name as your nickname when you connect. Please do not share the server IP with anybody outside of the Houston ARTCC.

Website

Upon receiving this email, you should be able to sign in on https://zhuartcc.org using VATSIM Connect. Here you can access controlling files and facility documents, the training center, event shift bookings, and see controlling statistics.

Getting Trained

The training center, located at https://zhuartcc.org/training, is the one-stop shop for all of your training needs. Here you can request training, view booked training sessions, and see past training notes. Instructions on how to schedule a session can be found in the Training Center.
